We are the American branch of AYUDH, the international youth group of Embracing the World, an organization spearheaded by humanitarian leader Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, affectionately known as Amma. As a part of AYUDH, youth between the ages of 14 - 30 are committed to generating a future of hope peace and social engagement while maintaining an awareness of spiritual principles. Come, be a part of this movement towards global peace and change!
